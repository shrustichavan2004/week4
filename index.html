About Us

We are a group of environmentalists and educators committed to spreading awareness about soil conservation. Our goal is to educate people about the importance of soil and how we can protect it for future generations.

Main Causes of Soil Erosion

Deforestation

Removal of trees increases runoff and reduces soil stability.

Overgrazing

Excessive grazing removes vegetation and exposes soil to erosion.

Unscientific Farming

Poor farming techniques like monoculture and excessive plowing degrade soil quality.

Industrialization

Chemical waste and construction destroy natural soil cover and structure.

Methods of Soil Conservation

Afforestation

Planting trees to prevent soil erosion and improve soil fertility.

Terrace Farming

Constructing terraces on slopes to reduce runoff and soil erosion.

Contour Ploughing

Ploughing along the contour lines to reduce water runoff and soil loss.

Crop Rotation

Rotating crops to maintain soil nutrients and prevent degradation.
